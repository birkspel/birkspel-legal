# Privacy Policy for Birk

_Last updated: November 2025_

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile application **Birk** (“the App”) handles data.  
The App is developed and published by an independent developer based in Sweden (“the Developer”).  
Contact: **birkspel@gmail.com**

---

## 1. Overview

The App collects only limited, non-personal information required for functionality and subscription management.  
No personal details such as name, email, or location are collected.

---

## 2. Data Collected

The App stores a random device identifier together with limited usage information, such as last active date, subscription status, and game progress.  
This data is securely stored on servers controlled by the Developer and used only to maintain functionality and manage subscriptions.  

The App also communicates with **RevenueCat** to manage subscriptions and verify purchases.  
No personal or sensitive data is shared or sold.

---

## 3. Data Retention and Security

Data is retained only as long as necessary to provide the App’s core features and subscription functionality.  
The Developer uses secure storage and industry-standard methods to protect the data.

If you wish to have data linked to your device ID deleted, you can contact the Developer at **birkspel@gmail.com**.

---

## 4. Third-Party Services

The App uses **RevenueCat** to handle in-app subscriptions and purchases.  
RevenueCat may process limited purchase-related information required by the App Stores’ payment systems.  
RevenueCat’s Privacy Policy:  
[https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy)

---

## 5. Legal Basis and Rights (GDPR)

Because the App is available in the European Union, it complies with the **General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)**.  

You may contact the Developer to:
- Request information about data linked to your device.
- Request deletion of such data.

These rights apply only to the pseudonymous data described above.

---

## 6. Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for general audiences and does not collect any personal data from users of any age.
